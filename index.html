Welcome!

This site is about the fantasy tabletop miniature wargame Hordes of the Things.

If you are familiar with Hordes of the Things we recommend you to take a look at the Community and Contacts to see if you can find some new material or groups to enjoy. You are of course welcome to enjoy our gallery.

If you are new to Hordes of the things in particular or tabletop miniature wargaming in general, please inform yourself in the About section.

Hordes of the Things - the small game with the huge feeling!

What do I mean by that?

The ruleset is minimal, the playing surface is small, the time requirement is around 45 minutes and the model count (the number of miniatures needed to play the game) is small.

At the same time the game gives the feeling of large battles, sweeping movement of large bodies of troops, and a true ebb and flow of battle.

Feel welcome to look around on the site and see if you get some inspiration!
